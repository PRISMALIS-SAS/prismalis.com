EUDR Connectivity to TRACES NT

PRISMALIS provides an API-driven validation and submission service to TRACES-NT for EUDR due diligence statements. The service validates payloads and submits electronic Due Diligence Statements into the EU official system.

  • API endpoints to validate DDS payloads before submission.
  • Automated DDS submission with status feedback and error handling.
  • Summer 2026 update planned for simplification and revised deadlines.

Research and Innovation

PRISMALIS' R&D effort is aligned with the most advanced needs identified in recent DPP and ESPR ecosystem work, focusing on functional building blocks that make Digital Product Passports and sustainability data spaces viable at industrial scale. The team works on trust and identity mechanisms, data standardization, product data modeling, interoperability frameworks, and scalable data infrastructures to support long-term deployment across sectors.

  • Trust and identity mechanisms: Robust trust frameworks for DPP, including integration with emerging EU digital identity wallets, verifiable credentials, and links to certified entities.
  • Data standardization and reliability: Harmonized, verifiable calculation methodologies, especially for environmental indicators, with proof and auditability mechanisms.
  • Product modeling and data granularity: Suitable granularity levels (model, lot, individual product), including support for complex and fungible products aligned with DPP requirements.
  • Semantic and technical interoperability: Common vocabularies, semantic models, and mapping to industrial standards so DPP data remains portable across systems.
  • Data infrastructures and scaling: Distributed, sovereign architectures that connect DPP platforms with EU data spaces while ensuring performance, security, and SME accessibility.
